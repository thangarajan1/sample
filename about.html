About Us

Our Commitment to Diversity:

At Karma Infra and Paving, we believe that diversity drives innovation and success. We are proud to be a minority-led company that champions diversity in all aspects of our business. We are committed to:

Creating Opportunities: Providing training and career advancement opportunities for individuals from diverse backgrounds.

Fostering Inclusion: Building a workplace culture where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives.

Partnering with Minority-Owned Businesses: Collaborating with minority-owned suppliers and subcontractors to support economic growth and sustainability within our communities.

Company History

Founded in 2023, Karma Infra and Paving has grown to become a leading minority and diversity-focused contractor in the central Alabama region. Our dedication to excellence, diversity, and customer engagement sets us apart in the industry.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide superior Infrastructure services while promoting diversity, inclusion, and customer engagement. We envision a future where infrastructure development is equitable and benefits every individual who has the ability to perform projects without limitation on Funding, Management and Backoffice support systems.
